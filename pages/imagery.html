Borders

Main Text Areas:

  • Border: Dashed black (#3A3A3A) 2 pixels
  • Background: White
  • Border Radius: 20 pixels

This dashed borders and rounded edges give off feelings of playfulness, fun and safety. It gives the appearance of a cut out activity for children. The drop shadoow brings the page to life with a 3D appearance.

Other Page Borders:

  • Solid Black (#3A3A3A) 2px

Buttons

  • Border: Solid Black (#3A3A3A) 2 pixels
  • Background: Yellow (#FFD954)
  • Border Radius: 4 pixels
  • Drop Shadow: 5px 5px 5px Gray (#808080)

Buttons are designed with sharper yet still rounded corners and fit with the bee motif of BeeBoo which matches the mascot and overall theme. The buttons have a drop shadow applied so the page does not appear flat. Button heirarchy can be found on the Colors section under Application.

Images & Image Style

Vector Images:

Vector images used on the BeeBoo site will feature colourful depictions of bee related activities and children interacting with bee's. These images will remain vibrant and playful through out the site highlighting apiarist related items and bee related themes. Images should have rounded corners in according with the 4px standards and a drop shadow consistent with the page elements listed above.

Real Images:

Real images will be used sparingly through out BeeBoo. When real images are used they should remain specific to apiary or cute bees doing cute things. All real photo's should high quality and promote professional apiary or fun bee content. The purpose of real photos is to either educate by showing real apiarists or show cuteness of bee's to build children's bonds with them and nature.
