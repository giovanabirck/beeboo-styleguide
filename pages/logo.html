LOGO

The BeeBoo logo is a stylized B that evokes the shape and colours of Bees. During the creation process it was recognized that a letter B and a Bee share a similar basic shape. The brown was chosen to soften the overall image and the yellow used is the main yellow from the colour guide.

Spacing

Online the logo should fit within a 100 x 100 container and have 16px of margin on the container.
